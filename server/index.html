What is the teaching chain?

Teaching chain (JLL) is an open source decentralized education industry agreement based on block chaining technology. The teaching chain uses the JLL intelligent protocol to introduce the block chain technology into the education industry, subvert the traditional educational industry and create the world's first educational public chain. The future blueprint includes a number of distributed DAPP applications in the educational field, a public chain of educational industry, and a central exchange of talent content.

Teaching chain tribe

The whole ecosystem of teaching chain consists of five elements: user, cognitive lecture hall, teaching chain community, mining and ranking. Users form a teaching chain community, which can acquire teaching chain tokens through mining mechanism, and other tokens of other projects that have strategic cooperation with the teaching chain. Mining is a way to acquire chain tokens and an important part of the whole ecosystem.

Teaching chain (JLL) DAPP application

Teachers and training institutions carry out all kinds of teaching content through JLL protocol, such as curriculum, books, audio and other upper chain certification. The teaching organization can find and purchase teaching content provided by teachers through JLL protocol and its certification; talents can also enjoy various kinds of JLL and various kinds of teachers and training institutions. Content of courses? All the transactions, training and teaching content information are stored by the distributed book. The enterprise can send out the verification request to the talented person, obtain all kinds of information recorded on the platform, obtain the comprehensive picture related to its education, and provide the decision support for the selection of its personnel.

JLL talent, content exchange

Based on the JLL protocol, the massive talents and their accreditation data, teachers and their uploaded teaching contents are given. Through JLL ecology, the transaction of talents and employers based on the data of talent education can be realized, based on the authentication, management, hatching of the content of teacher's personal IP, and the copyright transactions with the publishers, educational institutions and capital. When the above transactions reach enough, teaching chain talents and content exchanges can also be implemented.
